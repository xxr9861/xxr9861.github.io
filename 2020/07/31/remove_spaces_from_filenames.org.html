Remove spaces from filenames

When you download files from the internet, they usually have spaces in

filenames. The spaces can get annoying. Here is a script to remove the

whitespaces. 


#! /bin/bash
# filename: removespaces
prog(){
    for file in *
    do
	target="$(echo "$file" | sed -e 's/ /_/g' -e 's/[()]/_/g')"
	mv "$file" "$target"
    done
}
prog
